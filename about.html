About

I'm John, a digital product designer, experience strategist, writer, mentor, dad, tinkerer, bicycle builder and perpetual learner. Most of my work involves some form of user research, understanding the business, designOps, problem framing, negotiation, planning, designing, experimenting, testing, measuring, learning and iterating informed by said learnings.

I nerd out on things like breaking down and understanding systems, figuring out why one product resonates more than another and determining methods for calculating human response and perception towards products, brands and services.
