Snagajob Scheduling App

Mobile iOS / Android

Snagajob is the largest network for hourly job seekers and employers. While there, I worked with a small team called “The Connections Team” to define scope, analyze competitors, map out, design, prototype and test several concepts for a scheduling app that would be offered to employees and their workers (separate app) as part of extending SaJs product ecosystem. One of the first concepts that came out of this 4 month project was two separate native Scheduling a applications that both employers and job seekers could use to manage their respective schedules.
